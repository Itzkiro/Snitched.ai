# SC-01 U.S. House 2026 GOP Primary — Challenger Opposition Research

**District:** South Carolina's 1st Congressional District (Lowcountry — Charleston, Beaufort, Berkeley, Dorchester, Hilton Head, Mount Pleasant).
**Primary date:** June 9, 2026.
**Incumbent:** Rep. Nancy Mace (R) — running for SC Governor, not seeking re-election.
**Field:** 13–14 Republican candidates.
**Research compiled:** 2026-04-22 for Snitched.ai corruption research platform.

## 1. Tyler Dykes — Bluffton, SC

### Bio snapshot
- Age 26–27 (b. ~1998/1999). Lives in Bluffton, Beaufort County.
- Joined USMC 2017; discharged May 2023 under "other than honorable conditions" for participating in extremist activity (Charlottesville).
- Federal J6 defendant; pardoned by Trump Jan 2025.
- Campaign tagline: "Pardoned J6 Patriot."

### A. Public positions on Israel / Gaza / Iran / Palestine
**Openly anti-Israel-aid; isolationist / "America First" in the Tucker Carlson / MTG mold.** On his campaign site (votetylersc.com) he explicitly lists:
- "Cut all foreign aid — including to Israel and Ukraine."
- "BAN AIPAC, make them register as a foreign agent."
- "Pass a new War Powers Resolution Act — to get us OUT of the Iran War."
- "End all involvement in the Middle East."

In a March 2026 Live 5 News candidate profile he said the U.S. is wasting "political capital" on Israel, Gaza, Ukraine and Russia and that the Trump administration wrongly used "political capital to do strikes on Iran." Quote: "America needs to mind our own business and stay out of the business of the whole rest of the world."

Sources:
- https://votetylersc.com/ (campaign issues page)
- https://www.live5news.com/2026/03/01/we-palmetto-meet-candidate-tyler-dykes-sc-01/

### B. Connections to Israel-lobby groups
**None — actively hostile.** He calls for AIPAC to register under FARA. No AIEF/AIPAC trips, no RJC affiliation, no CUFI speakerships. He is the clearest anti-Israel-lobby voice in the field.

### C. Scandals / investigations / criminal record
Most documented of any candidate in the field.

- **Jan 6 federal conviction:** Pleaded guilty April 2024 to two felony counts of assaulting, resisting, or impeding officers (18 U.S.C. § 111). Sentenced July 22, 2024 by Judge Beryl Howell to **57 months federal prison**, 36 months supervised release, **$22,000 in fines/restitution**. DOJ: stole a police riot shield and used it twice against officers; allegedly performed a Nazi salute on the Capitol steps (Dykes disputes, says it was a "wave").
- **Trump pardon Jan 2025** — released early.
- **Virginia state conviction 2023:** Pleaded guilty in May 2023 to a felony charge of burning an object with intent to intimidate (tiki-torch march at UVA, Aug 11 2017 — the night before Unite the Right). Sentenced to **6 months** in VA state prison.
- **USMC separation:** Discharged "other than honorable" May 2023 for participating in extremist behavior (Charlottesville).

### D. Occupation / industry ties
Self-described on campaign site: "lost my business, been fired from my job." Full-time candidate/activist. No disclosed employer, no industry ties, no known real estate / defense / pharma / finance connections. Early fundraising trivial (~$20 reported on a $35K crowdfund goal per Post & Courier).

### E. Endorsements
None of note. No Trump, no Club for Growth, no Heritage Action, no RJC, no pro-Israel PACs. His only "validation" is the Trump pardon itself, which he campaigns on.

### Snitched signal summary
- **Israel-aid stance:** Hard opposed; wants AIPAC registered as foreign agent.
- **J6 stance:** Participated, convicted, pardoned, campaigns on it.
- **Biggest red flag:** Violent J6 felon + 2017 Charlottesville tiki-torch marcher with white-nationalist ties.

## 2. Mark Sanford — Dale, SC (Beaufort County)

### Bio snapshot
- Age 65.
- Former SC-01 U.S. Rep (1995–2001; 2013–2019).
- Former SC Governor (2003–2011).
- 2020 long-shot presidential primary challenger to Trump.
- Founded Norton and Sanford Real Estate Investment (1992).
- Filed for SC-01 just hours before March 30, 2026 deadline.

### A. Public positions on Israel / Gaza / Iran / Palestine
**Historically pro-Israel rhetorically, fiscally skeptical of Israel aid.** This is his most politically distinctive Israel-related fingerprint.

- **Aug 1, 2014:** One of only **8 House members (4R, 4D) to vote NO on H.R. 5249**, the $225 million emergency Iron Dome supplemental during the Gaza war. Sanford publicly defended the vote saying he opposed *unfunded emergency spending* ("skirting rules about paying for new spending") — not Israel's defense — and noted he had voted twice before to fund Iron Dome. The vote put him on nearly every pro-Israel-lobby watchlist for that cycle.
- **Supported HR 11 (2017)** reaffirming two-state solution and condemning UNSC 2334.
- **Opposed the 2015 JCPOA / Iran nuclear deal.**

Sources:
- https://www.postandcourier.com/politics/sanford-says-he-opposed-more-debt-not-israel-s-defense/article_5945c3af-0f40-5781-8a7b-df3d482e1458.html
- https://www.newsweek.com/full-list-representatives-voted-against-funding-israel-iron-dome-1632385
- https://ajcongress.org/jewishpoliticalguide/senators/mark-sanford/

### B. Connections to Israel-lobby groups
No documented AIEF/AIPAC Educational Foundation trip in public record. Not a known CUFI speaker. Not RJC-endorsed. Historically a libertarian-leaning fiscal conservative — AIPAC has never been enthusiastic about him since the 2014 Iron Dome "no."

### C. Scandals / investigations
**Extensive and ongoing.** Two separate affair scandals — 17 years apart.

1. **2009 "Hiking the Appalachian Trail" / Argentina affair & ethics case.**
   - June 2009: Disappeared from June 18–24, 2009; staff told press he was hiking the Appalachian Trail. Actually in Buenos Aires with mistress María Belén Chapur.
   - **Nov 18, 2009:** SC State Ethics Commission found probable cause on **37 ethics charges** — improper use of state-owned aircraft (Dept. of Natural Resources and SC Aeronautics Division), first/business-class flights on commercial, misuse of campaign funds.
   - **Settlement:** Sanford paid **$74,000 in civil fines ($2,000 per count)** — largest ethics fine in SC history at the time. Plus reimbursements: $18,000 to Commerce Dept., $7,792 to Aeronautics, $1,003 to DNR, $2,941 back to his campaign. Avoided criminal charges.
   - **Jan 2010:** SC House voted **102–11 to censure** him.
   - Dec 2009: Wife Jenny Sanford filed for divorce.
   - Feb 2013: Jenny Sanford filed **trespass complaint** — caught him sneaking into her Sullivan's Island home in violation of divorce terms. Became public during his 2013 comeback campaign.

2. **2025 Olivia Nuzzi affair allegation.**
   - November 2025: Olivia Nuzzi's ex-fiancé Ryan Lizza publicly accused Nuzzi of a 2020 affair with Sanford while she was covering his presidential primary run for New York Magazine.
   - Nuzzi's own memoir *American Canto* (Dec 2, 2025) references only the RFK Jr. entanglement; she *denied* the Sanford affair via attorney.
   - Vanity Fair subsequently let her contract expire at year-end 2025.
   - Story has trailed Sanford into the 2026 comeback.

- https://www.fitsnews.com/2026/04/03/mark-sanfords-latest-comeback-to-begin-in-dorchester-county/

### D. Occupation / industry ties
**Real estate.** Norton and Sanford Real Estate Investment (founded 1992). Also owned a Beaufort-area plantation. Published a 2021 book on political beliefs. Has been largely out of public life since 2020.

### E. Endorsements (2026)
None publicly announced as of filing. Trump is "widely expected to weigh in *against* Sanford" per FITSNews — the same Trump-backed primary challenge that beat him in 2018. Previous historical endorsers (2013): FreedomWorks, Nikki Haley, John Boehner, Ron Paul, Rand Paul, Tim Scott, Lindsey Graham — almost none of which translate to 2026 given his anti-Trump 2020 run.

### Snitched signal summary
- **Israel-aid stance:** Mixed — pro-Israel two-state rhetoric, but voted against emergency Iron Dome funding in 2014 on fiscal grounds.
- **J6 stance:** Has publicly criticized Trump and Jan 6; not a J6-apologist.
- **Biggest red flag:** 37 state ethics charges, $74K fine, censure — plus a second affair scandal resurfacing in 2025.

## 3. Alex Pelbath — Mount Pleasant, SC

### Bio snapshot
- Retired USAF Lt. Col.; 22 years service; 4,000+ flight hours, 1,000+ combat.
- Air Mission Commander for the final Kabul evacuation Aug 2021; personally flew the last U.S. military plane out of Afghanistan.
- U.S. Air Force Academy grad; former Division I wrestler; MBA (Strategic Leadership).
- Pentagon tour: helped draft two national defense budgets; advised Chairman of the Joint Chiefs.
- Currently a **Southwest Airlines pilot** (post-retirement civilian job — confirmed via FITSNews and LinkedIn).
- Entered race August 2025 — second Republican to file.

### A. Public positions on Israel / Gaza / Iran / Palestine
**Not explicit on Israel/Gaza — standard "peace through strength" Trump-conservative framing.** Campaign materials stress border/immigration/"anti-woke" military, not Middle East specifics. In a FITSNews studio interview (Nov 26, 2025) he praised Trump's "peace through strength" approach. On Iran he has spoken about Congressional oversight of the president's authority to use force in Iran/Venezuela without new AUMF — a mild reassertion of Article I war powers, but not an isolationist line.

No reviewed source records him calling Gaza operations genocide, conditioning aid, or criticizing Israel. Given his Pentagon career and defense-budget background, baseline assumption: pro-Israel-aid hawk until he says otherwise.

Sources:
- https://www.fitsnews.com/2025/11/26/in-studio-sc-1-congressional-candidate-alex-pelbath/
- https://www.live5news.com/2026/01/23/we-palmetto-meet-candidate-alex-pelbath-sc-01/
- https://www.foxnews.com/politics/first-fox-retired-air-force-colonel-piloted-last-flight-afghanistan-reveals-new-mission

### B. Connections to Israel-lobby groups
**No direct ties surfaced** — but he's a fresh first-time candidate and AIPAC's primary targeting typically kicks in once a candidate is viable. No AIEF trip record (he only retired recently). No CUFI speakerships. No RJC endorsement in public record. His Pentagon and defense-budget résumé is exactly the profile AIPAC courts.

### C. Scandals / investigations
**None found in public record.** Clean military career, no court records, no ethics issues surfaced in Post & Courier / FITSNews / SC Daily Gazette / Ballotpedia reviews. This is notable for an SC-01 field otherwise full of baggage.

### D. Occupation / industry ties
- Southwest Airlines commercial pilot (current).
- Military-adjacent / defense-aerospace credibility through 22-year USAF career and Pentagon budget role. No disclosed defense-contractor board seats or lobbyist work.
- No real estate, pharma, or finance ties surfaced.

### E. Endorsements
- Describes himself as a "Trump Conservative" and has pledged loyalty to Trump, but **no Trump endorsement** is documented in the SC-01 primary.
- No Club for Growth, Heritage Action, RJC, or pro-Israel super PAC endorsements found.
- Leading fundraiser and co-front-runner with Mark Smith (Pelbath 29.5% in the April 17, 2026 GOP forum straw poll; Polymarket predictions market had him at ~41.5%).

Sources:
- https://ballotpedia.org/Alex_Pelbath
- https://abcnews4.com/news/local/mark-smith-leads-sc-01-gop-forum-straw-poll-at-341-edging-alex-pelbath-congress-election-midterms-donald-trump-gop-republican-south-carolina

### Snitched signal summary
- **Israel-aid stance:** Presumed pro-aid / hawkish (Pentagon + "peace through strength") but not explicit.
- **J6 stance:** No J6 involvement; not a known apologist.
- **Biggest red flag:** Light on specific-policy public record — thin accountability surface, typical first-time "war hero" candidate pattern. Defense-budget-drafting Pentagon résumé is the most likely corruption-adjacent exposure going forward.

## 4. Mark (Marvin M.) Smith — Mount Pleasant / Daniel Island, SC

### Bio snapshot
- Age 55 (b. June 18, 1970, Bamberg, SC).
- **Businessman, not the Mount Pleasant mayor.** Owner/president of **McAlister-Smith Funeral Homes**, **Avinger Funeral Home**, and **Palmetto Cremation Society** (5 locations across Charleston & Berkeley counties).
- Former **Mount Pleasant Town Council** member (2013–2017).
- **SC House of Representatives, District 99** (Mount Pleasant / Daniel Island / Cainhoy / Hanahan / Goose Creek), since Nov 9, 2020 — currently mid-3rd term.
- Leading the SC-01 GOP forum straw poll at **34.1%** (April 17, 2026).
- High school prom date of Nikki Haley (per Post & Courier profile).

### A. Public positions on Israel / Gaza / Iran / Palestine
**Aligned with House GOP leadership / Trump administration line — no daylight.** At campaign launch he stated: "I support the President's position and the Speaker of the House's position on our foreign policies, and we will fight very hard to make sure we continue to keep our citizens safe." That is effectively a Johnson/Trump-aligned pro-Israel-aid stance by default — military aid for Israel, hawkish on Iran, no conditioning.

No record of him calling Gaza operations genocide. No record of dissenting Iron Dome/Israel-aid statements. SC House Israel resolutions passed during his tenure (H.4923 Support for Israel 2023–24; S.4352 Enduring Friendship 2025–26; H.4865 Judea and Samaria 2025–26) are routinely unanimous — no record of Smith voting against any.

Sources:
- https://www.postandcourier.com/politics/mark-smith-sc-district-nancy-mace/article_0aa9ec9a-5c4a-4bdc-9fc6-268a98fdc048.html
- https://www.counton2.com/news/your-local-election-hq/gop-state-rep-mark-smith-announces-bid-for-south-carolinas-1st-congressional-district/
- https://votemarksmith.com/

### B. Connections to Israel-lobby groups
No AIEF trip on public record. No RJC endorsement documented. No CUFI speakership documented. His leadership-aligned posture plus state-rep support for pro-Israel resolutions makes him a likely AIPAC-welcome profile, but no direct tie established yet.

### C. Scandals / investigations
**Two active / recent ethics issues — the highest-probability 2026 liability:**

1. **March 2026 — SC state-House-email campaign-fundraising violation (ongoing).**
   - Smith emailed all SC House members a fundraising invitation from his official MarkSmith@schouse.gov account — subject "Personal Invitation: My Fundraiser for Congress – Tuesday, March 3rd in Columbia (featuring Speaker Murrell Smith)." Contribution levels $250–$3,500.
   - **Violates S.C. Code § 8-13-765**, which bars using "government personnel, equipment, materials, or an office building in an election campaign."
   - Smith asked House Ethics Committee chairman Jay Jordan for an advisory opinion March 10, 2026 — the same day FITSNews exposed the email. Smith says the email was sent "inadvertently" from his iPhone.
   - Source: https://www.fitsnews.com/2026/03/11/sc-house-member-illegally-fundraises-on-state-email-account/

2. **2017 — LLC campaign-donation arrangement (Mount Pleasant Town Council reporting).**
   - Post & Courier reporting: Smith's town council campaign disclosures showed donations from multiple LLCs he personally controlled — including two headquartered at his home address — to his own campaign. Legal under SC law, but described as "out of the ordinary" and used as a workaround to contribution caps / attribution.
   - Source: https://www.postandcourier.com/politics/mount-pleasant-candidate-mark-smith-uses-llcs-to-support-his/article_efcf1aec-cbb1-11e7-9d52-1b2404f1ad01.html

3. **2011 civil suit — *Smith v. McAlister-Smith Funeral Home Inc* (D.S.C.).** Federal court dismissed the plaintiff's complaint without prejudice in 2012; low-severity. Not a Mark Smith-initiated suit or finding against him personally; included for completeness.

### D. Occupation / industry ties
- **Funeral services industry** — owner/operator of a 5-location funeral / cremation chain (McAlister-Smith, Avinger, Palmetto Cremation Society). Primary business interest.
- Real estate via personal LLCs used to route campaign contributions (2017 reporting).
- State-legislator role gives him a voting record on SC business/tax/health policy.
- No direct defense, pharma, or finance industry employment surfaced.

### E. Endorsements
- **Former U.S. Sen. Rick Santorum (PA)** — named on his own campaign site.
- **State Sen. Brian Adams (SC)**.
- Former U.S. Reps **Henry Brown** and **Tommy Hartnett** (both former SC-01 holders).
- **No Trump endorsement** documented — Smith has said Trump's support is one he "hopes to earn"; his launch video invokes Trump/Trump imagery ≥5 times.
- No RJC, Club for Growth, Heritage Action, or pro-Israel super PAC endorsements found.

Sources:
- https://votemarksmith.com/
- https://ballotpedia.org/Mark_Smith_(South_Carolina)
- https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mark_Smith_(South_Carolina_politician)

### Snitched signal summary
- **Israel-aid stance:** Defaults to Speaker/Trump position — pro-military-aid for Israel, hawkish on Iran.
- **J6 stance:** No direct J6 involvement; pro-Trump alignment but not an extremist.
- **Biggest red flag:** March 2026 state-email campaign-fundraising violation under S.C. Code § 8-13-765 (open ethics matter); pattern of LLC-self-donation going back to 2017 Mount Pleasant race.
