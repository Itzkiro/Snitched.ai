# FL-07 (U.S. House) 2026 — Candidate Investigation

District: Florida's 7th Congressional District (Seminole + Volusia counties — Sanford, Daytona, DeLand). R-leaning suburban / exurban district. Primary: August 18, 2026. General: November 3, 2026.

Snapshot of field (2026 cycle, per FEC + Ballotpedia):
- **Cory Mills** (R, incumbent) — embroiled in OCE/House Ethics investigation, DC police probe, restraining-order/revenge-porn lawsuit, and federal-contracting-while-in-office allegations.
- **Michael "Mike Don" Johnson** (R, primary challenger) — Vietnam-era Army vet, 36-year DoD program manager, "Reagan Republican." Lost FL-07 GOP primary to Mills in 2024 (~80.9% Mills vs. Johnson's ~19%).
- **Sarah Ulrich** (R, primary challenger) — 7th-generation Floridian, real-estate businesswoman from Port Orange, MBA from UF Warrington. First-time candidate. Anti-scandal "accountability" frame.
- **Marialana Kinter** (D, challenger) — Navy nuclear reactor plant supervisor 2016–2024 (now Reserves), born Bozeman MT, working-class background, attending UCF as of 2025. Explicitly anti-AIPAC, anti-genocide.

Anchor reporting/data: Ballotpedia, FEC filings, Track AIPAC (boughtbyzionism.org), Wikipedia, Florida Politics, Tampa Free Press, Florida Phoenix, The Hill, NBC Washington, Newsweek, Mediaite, Daily Beast, Roger Stone via Primetimer, Palestine Chronicle, ActBlue.

## 1. Cory Mills (R, incumbent)

### A. Israel / Gaza / Iran / Palestine
- **Voted NO** on H.R. 8034 (Israel Security Supplemental Appropriations Act, April 20, 2024 — $14.3B for Israel including Iron Dome / David's Sling). Among only 21 GOP NO votes nationally — bracketed with Matt Gaetz on the FL R side and Maxwell Frost on the D side. (Floridian Press / Florida Phoenix / Jewish Insider) Notable for an incumbent often coded "pro-Israel."
- **Pro-Israel public profile:** Mercury One / Glenn Beck partnership to evacuate ~257 Americans from Israel after Oct. 7, 2023 (98 first flight, 159 second flight). Received Bonhoeffer "Angel" Award. Repeated Glenn Beck Program appearances framing Hamas attacks in eschatological/biblical terms.
- **Co-sponsored & voted YEA** on H.Res. countering antisemitism (May 14, 2025).
- **No public position on conditioning aid, two-state solution, or FARA registration for AIPAC.** Has not publicly opposed AIPAC.
- **Iran:** Hawkish posture publicly; serves on House Foreign Affairs (Western Hemisphere subcommittee — not the Middle East subcommittee).

### B. America First posture & endorsements
- **Trump endorsement:** YES. Trump endorsed Mills' reelection on **Feb 11, 2026** ("America First Patriot," "Complete and Total Endorsement") — even amid scandals. Per Roger Stone (via Primetimer), Mills allegedly "lied to Trump about no restraining order against him to secure his endorsement."
- Roger Stone publicly attacking Mills suggests at least some MAGA-orbit hostility despite the Trump nod.
- House Freedom Caucus: not a confirmed member as of 2026 cycle (Wikipedia/Voter's Right do not list HFC affiliation).
- Foreign aid in general: voted against the April 2024 supplemental — reads as "America First" on the ledger, but PACEM Defense itself sells munitions abroad (Iraq, Ukraine, Colombia).

### C. Scandals / federal/state investigations / criminal history
- **Office of Congressional Ethics (OCE) investigation, August 2024:** Mills' companies (PACEM Defense / ALS) secured **~$1M** in federal munitions contracts since taking office, **94 contracts** awarded to entities he owns since Jan 9, 2024. Mills refused to provide tax returns. Constitutionally the Domestic Emoluments / federal-contractor statutes prohibit MoCs from holding federal contracts.
- **House Ethics Committee** announced investigation **March 27, 2025**; **Investigative Subcommittee formed November 2025** to probe fraud allegations and "conduct with women."
- **H.Res. 893 (119th Congress)**: Censure resolution to remove Mills from House Armed Services and House Foreign Affairs committees (he narrowly avoided a formal censure vote, per Tampa Free Press).
- **DC Police domestic-assault investigation (Feb 19, 2025):** Police responded 1:15 a.m. to penthouse at The Wharf. Alleged victim **Sarah Raviani** (co-founder, Iranians for Trump) reported Mills "grabbed her, shoved her, and pushed her out of the door"; officers documented fresh bruises; per Florida Politics' April 2026 records release, **DC officers wanted to arrest Mills and even summoned a transport vehicle**, but the U.S. Attorney's Office declined to sign the warrant. Raviani later recanted physical-abuse allegations (blamed bruises on medical/overseas activity). MPD's Internal Affairs is separately investigating how the case was handled.
- **Lindsey Langston "revenge-porn" suit (August 2025):** Miss United States 2024 sued alleging Mills threatened to release nude videos after their relationship ended (Nov 2021–Feb 2025). **October 2025 — Florida judge issued protective order through year-end 2025.**
- **Eviction (2025):** $20,833/month Maryland penthouse; $85,000+ in unpaid rent.
- **PACEM Defense munitions plant (Taylor County, FL):** Closed twice by FL DFS for failing to pay workers' comp insurance premiums (Florida Phoenix, March 2026). Tear gas previously used on BLM protesters in 2020 (per Politico/Newsweek 2022).
- **Bronze Star dispute:** Allegations Mills mischaracterized the circumstances of his Bronze Star.
- **Wife / ex-wife Rana Al Saadi:** Iraqi refugee (arrived 2008), naturalized, served in Trump admin national-security role. Married 2014 at Dar Al-Hijrah Islamic Center (Falls Church, VA) by Imam **Mohammed Al-Hanooti** — late Palestine-born cleric whom FBI accused of raising **$6M for Hamas** and listed as unindicted co-conspirator in the **1993 World Trade Center bombing** plot. Mills' explanation: only Iraqi imam Al Saadi's mother could find willing to sign. Mills says they have been separated 3 years, in divorce proceedings 2.5 years. **Five former colleagues told Blaze News** in May 2025 that Mills had told them he converted to Islam (girlfriend Raviani disputed).

### D. Donors & industry ties
- **PACEM Solutions / PACEM Defense / ALS Less-Lethal Systems** — defense contractor he founded; ~$10M+ revenue. Self-dealing concern: voting on Armed Services + Foreign Affairs while company holds federal munitions contracts. **2015 Iraq deal: $228M** with U.K. partner. Sales to Ukraine and Colombia. **$48M debt** to Canadian lender (per 2023 reporting).
- Defense PACs and arms-export-aligned donors.
- **Pro-Israel money (Track AIPAC):** Mills has received **~$36,573** in Israel-lobby PAC money (relatively modest) — donors include AIPAC, RJC, AMP, USI. **Not lockstep AIPAC** given his April 2024 Israel-aid NO vote.
- FL real estate.

### E. Endorsements (as of April 2026)
- Trump (Feb 11, 2026) — confirmed
- Pro-Israel PACs: AIPAC, RJC, AMP, USI ($36,573 cumulative)
- Roger Stone has publicly accused Mills of misleading Trump — directional indicator that the Stone wing is hostile.
- No confirmed Heritage Action / Club for Growth / Freedom Caucus PAC endorsements visible as of April 2026.

## 2. Marialana Kinter (D, challenger)

### A. Israel / Gaza / Iran / Palestine
- **Explicitly rejects AIPAC money.** ActBlue page literally titled "Marialana Kinter stands against genocide and rejects AIPAC!" — frames the campaign around "challenging AIPAC's scandal-plagued Rep. Cory Mills."
- Featured in **Palestine Chronicle's** "Falling from Grace: Who Are the US Candidates Refusing AIPAC Money?" roundup.
- Aligned with the **Reject AIPAC** progressive coalition.
- Specific Iran / two-state / FARA-registration positions are not yet on the campaign site, which leans biographical/working-class. But the anti-genocide framing is unambiguous.

### B. America First / general posture
- Working-class, Medicaid-SNAP-school-lunch-raised, foster-care background, single-mom upbringing — frames an economic-populist Democrat. Not establishment.
- Critical of Pete Hegseth's treatment of the military (per Kate-for-the-People Substack interview).
- Listed by **Take Back Congress** — anti-corporate-money slate.

### C. Scandals / investigations / criminal history
- None found. Navy 2016–2024 served honorably as Nuclear Machinist Mate, qualified as Nuclear Reactor Plant Supervisor (USS Nimitz reactor plant supervisor + QA + DC lead). No publicly-reported military discipline.

### D. Donors & industry ties
- Grassroots **ActBlue** small-dollar.
- Veteran community.
- Take Back Congress (progressive slate).

### E. Endorsements (as of April 2026)
- No major formal endorsements yet documented. Listed on Take Back Congress / Candidates of Our Dreams (Kate-for-the-People).
- Refuses AIPAC, RJC, NORPAC, UDP, DMFI on the record.

### B. America First posture & endorsements
- Self-describes as "America First" and "Freedom Caucus"-aligned candidate, but **no Freedom Caucus PAC endorsement is documented.**
- Lost 2024 GOP primary to Mills with ~19% (Mills 80.9%) — already on second cycle.
- 36-year Department of Defense program manager (multi-billion-dollar acquisition programs) — likely sympathetic to defense-industrial-base posture.
- Trump endorsement: **NO** (Trump endorsed Mills).

### C. Scandals / investigations / criminal history
- None found. Vietnam-era Army vet (1973–1976). Southern Illinois University BA (1983), Webster University grad (1999). Previously ran for Florida State Senate in 2022.

### D. Donors & industry ties
- Self-funded / small-dollar — minimal FEC activity.
- Background in DoD acquisition / "fourth estate" agencies.

### E. Endorsements (as of April 2026)
- None confirmed. Pledged to support **U.S. Term Limits**.
